About Us

Urban Construction Limited, we are based in West London carrying out construction services, property maintenance and installation work across London. We provide the complete solution from end to end for house improvement which includes a loft conversion, refurbishment, property maintenance, carpentry, plastering, tilling floors/walls, brick & block work, painting & decorating, bathroom & kitchen installation, porches and heating complete system. Our skillful experts pride themselves on being respectful, highly professional, efficient and always meeting deadlines. We deliver a first class finish to every project with thorough attention to detail. Our professional construction team, who come supported by a dedicated workforce, are well regarded by commercial and residential property developers.
